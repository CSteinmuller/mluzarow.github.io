MethHelper


MethHelper is a bonus utility for use on any missions which involve the cooking of the "meth" loot. Particularly useful for filtering through Bain's unhelpful ingredient dialogue in levels such as Cook Off.

This mod prints information to the left-hand side of the screen regarding the status-bar of the current batch of meth, including current stage, ingredient, and feedback that it has been added.

Mod works on Cook Off, Rats day 1, Dockyard, and Hotline Miami day 1.

This mod requires Payday 2 BLT, a Lua hook which you can get here.

No Bag Bendy Tilt


No Bag Bendy Tilt corrects for the player camera rotation, the "dutch angle" effect, present upon picking up bagged loot. Simple and direct.
